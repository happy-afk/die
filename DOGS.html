DOGS SCIENCE

Dogs lap, or take up
the water with their tongues curled backward
as they lap
they move their tongues very quickly to build up momentum
that forces the water into a column and up into their mouths
the Virginia Tech researchers filmed 19 dogs of different sizes and breeds as they drank water.

In dogs
fungal infections can occur due to contact with other animals, exposure within the environment
or as a result of the overgrowth of fungi (yeast) that are naturally present in and on the dog's own body.

They possess up to 300 million olfactory receptors in their noses
compared to about six million in us
and the part of a dog's brain that is devoted to analyzing smells is about 40 times greater than ours.
